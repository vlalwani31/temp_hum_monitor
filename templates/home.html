Welcome to our EC 463 Mini Project's Home Page


Our project utilizes SSO from google. The person can login from clicking the Login tab and the person can logout from clicking the Logout tab. If a person is not logged in and tries to click on the Temperature or Humidity tab, then they get redirected to the Login tab. After Logging in, the person can access the information on the temperature and the humidity tab. Even after closing the tab without logging out, when the person returns to the web application, he doesn't needs to login again since the person didn;t logout previouly.
